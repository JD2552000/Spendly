Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2026

Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information Spendly ("we", "us") collects when you use our personal expense tracking application (the "Service"), how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Spendly, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide directly when you create an account and use the Service. This includes your account details, such as your name and email address, and the financial data you enter, such as expense amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We may also collect basic technical information, such as your browser type and general usage activity, to keep the Service running reliably.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the Service. This includes storing and displaying your expenses, generating category breakdowns and spending summaries, and letting you filter your activity by time period. We do not sell your personal or financial data, and we do not use it for advertising. We may use aggregated, anonymised information to understand how the Service is used and to make it better.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely and is retained only for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. You may request that your data be exported or permanently deleted at any time, and we will act on such requests within a reasonable period.

4. Third Party Services

We may rely on trusted third-party providers to host and operate the Service, such as infrastructure and database hosting. These providers only process your data on our behalf and are bound to keep it secure and confidential. We do not share your personal financial data with third parties for their own marketing purposes. Where the Service links to external sites, their own privacy policies apply.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, or if you would like to access, correct, or delete your personal data, please contact us. We will respond to your enquiry as promptly as we can. We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, and any changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
